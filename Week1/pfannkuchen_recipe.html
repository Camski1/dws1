Pfannkuchen (German Pancakes)

Recipe from my mom's recipe box.

Ingredients

Pancake

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 6 eggs
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Apple Topping

  • 3 pounds apples (cut into eighths)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions

  1. Set your oven at 400 degrees. Then take ¼ cup of butter and cook it in your heating oven in a 9in by 13 in pan.
  2. Add the flour, milk, eggs, salt, and sugar into a blender. Mix gently just until flour has been combined to the liquid.
  3. Add batter to hot buttered pan, and bake for 20- 25 min.
  4. As your pancake is cooking add all apple ingredients to a large pan.
  5. Cook over medium heat until apples are soft and liquid has gotten thick. (Stir Regularly)
  6. When both the pancake and apples are finished. Top the pancake with the apples.
